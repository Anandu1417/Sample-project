About us

Welcome to CarWise!

At CarWise, our mission is to empower car buyers and enthusiasts with genuine, user-generated car reviews and data-driven insights. We believe that the best car buying decisions come from having access to reliable information from real car owners.

Our Story:

CarWise was founded by a team of car enthusiasts and tech experts who saw the need for a reliable platform where car owners can share their experiences and potential buyers can benefit from this collective wisdom. By leveraging advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms, we analyze each review to provide clear sentiment classifications and insightful dashboard data.

What We Offer:

User Reviews: Honest opinions and experiences from real car owners.

Sentiment Analysis: Advanced NLP techniques to classify reviews into positive, negative, or neutral sentiments.

Interactive Dashboard: A comprehensive Power BI dashboard where users can compare different car brands on aggregated reviews.

Join Us:

Become part of the CarWise community. Your reviews help others make informed decisions, and your feedback helps us improve our platform. Together, we can make car buying smarter and more transparent.
